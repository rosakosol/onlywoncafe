Our Story

Only Won Cafe & Co was established in 2017 by Jon Woo, a Korean-born coffee addict. Located in the heart of Sydney CBD, we serve a diverse menu that includes Korean-inspired dishes paired with artisanal Guatemalan coffee.

Welcome to Only Won Cafe & Co, a haven of Korean-inspired delights nestled in the heart of Sydney, Australia. At Only Won, we invite you to embark on a culinary journey that seamlessly blends the rich traditions of Korean cuisine with the vibrant and diverse flavors of Sydney.


Our story begins with a passion for creating a unique space that goes beyond the ordinary, where every sip and bite is an experience to be savored. Inspired by the dynamic and eclectic nature of Korean culture, Only Won Cafe & Co is a celebration of flavors, aromas, and the art of brewing the perfect cup of coffee.

Step into our cozy haven, where modern aesthetics meet the warmth of Korean hospitality. Our inviting atmosphere is designed to make you feel at home, whether you're seeking a quiet corner for reflection or a lively spot to catch up with friends. The fusion of contemporary design elements and traditional Korean touches creates a harmonious ambiance that is both stylish and comforting.


At the heart of Only Won is our commitment to culinary excellence. Our menu is a carefully curated selection of Korean-inspired dishes, each crafted with precision and a dash of creativity. From traditional favorites like Bibimbap and Kimchi Pancakes to innovative twists on classic flavors, our chefs take pride in presenting a menu that caters to diverse palates.

Of course, no visit to Only Won is complete without exploring our specialty coffee offerings. We believe that a cup of coffee is an art form, and our baristas are dedicated to brewing perfection in every sip. Whether you prefer a velvety latte, a bold Americano, or a specialty Korean-inspired brew, our coffee menu promises a delightful exploration of flavors.

At Only Won, we are more than just a cafe; we are a community. Our team is passionate about fostering connections, and we invite you to join us in creating memories, one cup at a time. We are proud to be a part of the vibrant tapestry of Sydney's culinary scene, and we look forward to welcoming you to Only Won Cafe & Co.

Come, indulge in the beauty of Korean-inspired flavors, the warmth of our hospitality, and the joy of shared moments. Only Won Cafe & Co is not just a destination; it's an experience crafted with love and dedication.
